Lasagna

Description

This page contains the recpie for Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (500g) milk
  • 1 cup (500g) cream cheese
  • 1 cup (500g) shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup (500g) shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup (500g) shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. In a bowl, mix milk, cream cheese, shredded mozzarella, shredded cheddar cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese
  3. Place bowl in oven and bake for 45 minutes
  4. Remove bowl from oven and let cool
