Pasta

Description

This page contains the recpie for Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup chopped green chili
  • 1 cup chopped garlic
  • 1 cup chopped red pepper
  • 1 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place spinach, chopped tomatoes, green chili, garlic, red pepper, green pepper, spinach, cilantro in a bowl.
  3. Place bowl in oven and cook, stirring occasionally, until spinach is cooked through.
  4. Remove bowl from oven and let cool.
  5. Place bowl in oven and cook, stirring occasionally, until spinach is cooked through.
