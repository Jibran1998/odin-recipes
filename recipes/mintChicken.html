Mint Chicken

Description

This page contains the recpie for Mint Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mint puree
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup chicken stock

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a bowl, combine mint puree, chicken broth, and chicken stock.
  3. Bring to a boil.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Stir in mint puree.
  6. Cook, stirring occasionally, until mint puree is thickened.
  7. Serve with rice.
